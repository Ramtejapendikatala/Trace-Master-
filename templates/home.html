TraceXpert
Real-Time Facial Recognition and Case Management for Missing Person Recovery

This project addresses the alarming rise in missing persons cases, a global concern. Families suffer immense distress, and traditional search methods often fall short due to time constraints and lack of technological support. Recognizing the need for a more efficient and technology-driven solution, we developed this platform to assist in identifying and reuniting missing persons with their families.
